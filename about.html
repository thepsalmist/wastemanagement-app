About Us

Waste Master is a social enterprise that aims to revolutionize waste management, from collection all through to recycling.Our biggest motivation is creating a business model that offers solutions to the current environmental challenges that we face.

What We Do

Upon Sign Up on Waste Master, We collect your waste regularly as per your schedule,we take it to our local sites, from where we do secondary sorting. We then fulfill recycle requests from our manufacturing partners
